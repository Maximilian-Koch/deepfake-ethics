Real or AI? The Line is Blurring.

Deepfakes are synthetic media created using AI technology to manipulate or generate realistic multimedia content. They are often used to replace someone's likeness or voice in existing footage or to create entirely fabricated events. It allows for unseen ways of creativity and accessibility, but also for unseen amounts of misinformation, hatred and fraud. Major ethical dilemmas arise with implications for all internet users.

The Core Conflict:

Should we value Free Expression & Innovation, despite the risk of deception?

VS

Should we prioritize Truth, Safety, & Personal Dignity, even if it means limiting technology?

Ethical Lenses to Consider:

Preference Utilitarianism

Deontology
